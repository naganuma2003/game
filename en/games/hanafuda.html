Play a card from your hand to match a card of the same month on the field and capture both. Then flip a card from the deck and match again. Collect Brights, Animals, Ribbons, and Plains to form yaku (scoring combinations). When you complete a yaku, choose "Koi-Koi" to keep going for higher points, or "Stop" to bank your score. Koi-koi doubles your points if you win. Play the set number of months, and the highest total score wins.
