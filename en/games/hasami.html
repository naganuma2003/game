Hasami Shogi

HASAMI SHOGI — First player (Fu / You) is at the bottom
Fu captured 0/5 Your turn To captured 0/5
Rules: Pieces move like a rook — any number of squares horizontally or vertically (no jumping). Capture by flanking an opponent's piece(s) between two of yours in a line (captures entire group). Corner pieces are captured by covering both diagonal approaches. Moving into a flanked position yourself is safe. First to capture 5 wins.
