Nine Men's Morris

NINE MEN'S MORRIS
Mode:
Place 9 pieces each on the 24 intersections of three concentric squares. Form a mill (3 in a row along a line) to remove one of your opponent's pieces (pieces in a mill cannot be removed). After all pieces are placed, move them along the lines to adjacent points. Reduce your opponent to 2 pieces or block all their moves to win. When down to 3 pieces, you can "fly" to any empty point.
