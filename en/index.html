🎲 Backgammon Rules

Objective

Move all 15 of your pieces from your opponent's side to your home board (bottom-right 6 points), then bear them off the board first to win.

Basic Movement

  • Roll 2 dice and advance pieces by the number shown.
  • You can use both dice on separate pieces or combine on one piece.
  • Doubles let you use the same number 4 times.
  • You cannot land on a point occupied by 2+ opponent pieces (a stack).

Hit

  • Landing on a point with a single opponent piece (a blot) sends it to the bar.
  • A piece on the bar must re-enter the opponent's home board before any other move.

Bearing Off

  • Once all 15 pieces are in your home board, you can start bearing off.
  • Remove pieces matching the dice roll. If no piece matches, move the farthest piece.

Winning Points

  • Normal win: 1 point if opponent has borne off at least 1 piece.
  • Gammon: 2 points if opponent has borne off 0 pieces.
  • Backgammon: 3 points if opponent still has pieces on the bar or your home board.

🏝️ Catan Rules

Objective

Settle Catan island and be the first to reach 10 victory points.

Gameplay

  • Roll dice to produce resources from adjacent tiles.
  • Trade resources and build roads, settlements, cities, and development cards.
  • Rolling 7 activates the robber.

🀄 Mahjong Rules

Objective

Form a winning hand of 4 melds + 1 pair (14 tiles total). Must have at least one yaku (scoring pattern).

Gameplay

  • 4 players draw and discard tiles in turn.
  • Call chi/pon/kan to claim discards.
  • Declare riichi for bonus points when in tenpai (one tile away).

⚫ Othello Rules

Objective

Have more discs of your color on the 8x8 board when the game ends.

Rules

  • Place a disc to flank opponent's discs in any direction.
  • All flanked discs are flipped to your color.
  • Pass if no valid move. Game ends when both pass or board is full.

🟪 Tetris Rules

Basics

  • Rotate and move falling tetrominoes to fill complete rows.
  • Clearing 4 rows at once = Tetris (highest score).
  • Game over when pieces stack to the top.

VS Mode

  • Split-screen simultaneous play. Clearing 2+ lines sends garbage to opponent.

👾 Space Invaders Rules

Objective

Destroy all alien formations to defend Earth.

Controls

  • Arrow keys to move, Space to shoot.
  • Use shields for cover. Shoot UFOs for bonus points.

🔢 2048 Rules

Objective

Swipe tiles on a 4x4 board to merge matching numbers and create 2048.

Rules

  • Same numbers merge when they collide.
  • A new 2 or 4 appears after each move.
  • Game over when no moves remain.

🔎 Mastermind Rules

Objective

Guess the secret 4-digit number (all unique digits 0-9).

Feedback

  • Hit: correct digit in correct position.
  • Blow: correct digit in wrong position.

💣 Minesweeper Rules

Objective

Reveal all non-mine squares.

Rules

  • Numbers show adjacent mine count.
  • Right-click to flag suspected mines.
  • Click a mine = game over.

🦅 Hol's der Geier Rules

Objective

Collect the most points from score cards.

Rules

  • Score cards range from -5 to +10. Each player bids simultaneously.
  • Highest bid wins positive cards. Ties cancel out.
  • Lowest bid gets negative cards.

🃏 Algo Rules

Objective

Guess all of your opponent's hidden card numbers. Last player standing wins.

Rules

  • 24 cards (black 0-11, white 0-11) arranged in ascending order.
  • Draw a card, then guess an opponent's card. Correct = continue. Wrong = your drawn card is revealed.

🟥 Blokus Rules

Objective

Place as many tiles as possible. Fewest remaining squares wins.

Rules

  • Each piece must touch your own color at a corner but never along an edge.
  • First piece must cover your starting corner.

⚫ Gomoku Rules

Objective

Be the first to get exactly 5 stones in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) on a 15x15 board.

🪨 Mancala Rules

Objective

Have more seeds in your store when the game ends.

Rules

  • Pick up all seeds from one of your pits and sow counterclockwise.
  • Landing in your store = extra turn. Landing in an empty pit on your side = capture.

⬡ Hex Rules

Objective

Connect your two opposite sides with a chain of stones. No draws are possible.

👻 Geister Rules

Win Conditions

  • Capture all 4 of opponent's blue ghosts.
  • Move one of your blue ghosts to opponent's corner exit.
  • Opponent captures all 4 of your red ghosts.

🔴 Connect Four Rules

Objective

Connect 4 of your color in a row (vertical, horizontal, or diagonal) on a 7x6 grid.

Rules

  • Pieces drop to the lowest empty row in the chosen column.

🔴 Checkers Rules

Rules

  • Move diagonally forward. Jump over opponent pieces to capture (mandatory).
  • Multi-jumps are required. Reaching the back row promotes to King (moves both directions).

🔢 Slide Puzzle Rules

Objective

Slide tiles into numerical order (1,2,3...) with the blank in the bottom-right.

Sizes

3x3 (8-puzzle), 4x4 (15-puzzle), 5x5 (24-puzzle).

📦 Dots and Boxes Rules

Rules

  • Take turns drawing one line between dots.
  • Complete a box = claim it + draw again.
  • Most boxes wins.

✶ Chinese Checkers Rules

Objective

Move all your pieces from your triangle to the opposite triangle.

Movement

  • Step to an adjacent empty space, or jump over pieces (chain jumps allowed).

⊙ Otrio Rules

Win by any of 3 patterns

  • Same size: 3 same-sized rings in a row.
  • Concentric: S/M/L all in one cell.
  • Ascending: S→M→L diagonally.

🚧 Quoridor Rules

Objective

Reach the opposite side first.

Rules

  • Each turn: move your pawn 1 step, OR place a wall (10 walls each).
  • Walls must never completely block a path to the goal.

✂️ Hasami Shogi Rules

Objective

Capture 5 opponent pieces first by flanking them horizontally or vertically.

🃏 Solitaire (Klondike) Rules

Objective

Build 4 foundation piles by suit from Ace to King.

Rules

  • Tableau cards stack in alternating colors, descending.
  • Only Kings can fill empty columns.

🎴 Memory Match Rules

Rules

  • Flip 2 cards per turn. Match = keep them + go again.
  • Most pairs wins.

🂡 Blackjack Rules

Objective

Get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over.

Rules

  • Hit = draw a card. Stand = keep your hand. Double Down = double bet + 1 card.
  • A + face/10 = Blackjack (1.5x payout).

🃏 Daifugo Rules

Objective

Be the first to play all your cards.

Rules

  • Card strength: 3<4<...<K<A<2. Play higher cards or pass.
  • 8-cut: playing 8 clears the pile. Revolution: 4-of-a-kind reverses order.

🃆 Sevens Rules

Objective

Be the first to play all your cards.

Rules

  • 7s start on the table. Play cards adjacent to existing sequences.
  • Limited passes allowed.

♟ Shogi Rules

Objective

Checkmate the opponent's King.

Key Features

  • Captured pieces can be dropped back onto the board as your own.
  • Most pieces can promote when entering the opponent's territory (last 3 rows).

🃏 Texas Hold'em Rules

Objective

Make the best 5-card hand from 2 hole cards + 5 community cards.

Rounds

Preflop → Flop (3 cards) → Turn (4th) → River (5th) → Showdown.

Hand Rankings (low to high)

High Card → Pair → Two Pair → Three of a Kind → Straight → Flush → Full House → Four of a Kind → Straight Flush → Royal Flush

🎴 UNO Rules

Objective

Be the first to empty your hand. Call "UNO!" on your last card.

Action Cards

  • Skip, Reverse, Draw 2, Wild, Wild Draw 4.

🃏 Speed Rules

Objective

Empty your hand and draw pile first.

Rules

  • Both players play simultaneously (no turns). Play cards 1 above or below the pile card.

⚫ Go Rules

Objective

Surround more territory (empty intersections) than your opponent.

Rules

  • Black and white alternate placing stones. Stones with no liberties are captured.
  • Ko rule prevents infinite loops. Both pass = game ends.

♞ Chess Rules

Objective

Checkmate the opponent's King.

Special Rules

  • Castling, en passant, pawn promotion.

🎴 Hanafuda Koi-Koi Rules

Objective

Match cards by month to form scoring combinations (yaku).

Koi-Koi

  • When you complete a yaku, choose to stop (score now) or call koi-koi (continue for higher score, but risk opponent winning).

🔢 Sudoku Rules

Rules (just 3)

  • Each row must contain 1-9 exactly once.
  • Each column must contain 1-9 exactly once.
  • Each 3x3 box must contain 1-9 exactly once.

⊞ Nine Men's Morris Rules

Objective

Reduce opponent to 2 pieces or block all their moves.

Phases

  • Phase 1: Place pieces. Forming 3 in a row (mill) lets you remove an opponent piece.
  • Phase 2: Move pieces to adjacent points.
  • Phase 3: When down to 3 pieces, you can fly to any empty point.

🐮 6 Nimmt! Rules

Rules

  • Everyone plays a card simultaneously. Cards are placed in ascending order on 4 rows.
  • Playing the 6th card in a row = take all 5 cards as penalty points.

🦁 Dobutsu Shogi Rules

Win Conditions

  • Catch: Capture opponent's Lion.
  • Try: Move your Lion to opponent's back row.

Pieces

  • Lion (all directions), Elephant (diagonal), Giraffe (orthogonal), Chick (forward only, promotes to Hen).

🏰 Carcassonne Rules

Rules

  • Draw and place tiles matching edges. Optionally place a meeple on the new tile.
  • Completed roads (1pt/tile), cities (2pt/tile + crests), monasteries (9pt when surrounded).

⭕ Tic-Tac-Toe Rules

Rules

  • 3x3 grid. Get 3 in a row to win. All 9 filled with no winner = draw.

🎲 Chinchiro Rules

Rules

  • Roll 3 dice. Triples, straights (4-5-6 or 1-2-3), and pairs determine your score.
  • No valid hand = re-roll (up to 3 times).

🎲 Yacht Rules

Rules

  • Roll 5 dice up to 3 times per turn, keeping any between rolls.
  • Score in one of 12 categories each round. Yacht (5 of a kind) = 50 points.

💣 Bomberman Rules

Rules

  • Place bombs to destroy enemies. Collect power-ups: extra bombs, longer flames, speed.
  • 3 lives. Don't get caught in your own blast!

💀 Russian Roulette Rules

Rules

  • Gun mode: 6-chamber revolver, 1 bullet. Take turns pulling the trigger. Last one standing wins.
  • Bomb mode: 16 buttons, one is the bomb. 2-6 players.
